  1. How are the ratings distributed? Create both a histogram and a boxplot and describe them. Don’t forget to mention outliers, if there are any.
hist(choco$Rating, main="Frequency of chocolate ratings", xlab="Rating")

boxplot(choco$Rating, main="Boxplot of Chocolate Ratings", xlab="Rating", horizontal=TRUE)

Answer: The histogram shows that the graph is left skewed towards “Outstanding” Rating. The boxplot shows that the mean of the rating is somewhere between 3.0 and 3.5, which shows that the ratings of the chocolates are generally favorable from recommended to outstanding. The min of the rating is around 2.0 to 2.5. Ratings that are below 2.0 are generally considered outliers, so there are not many unpleasant chocolates in this graph
